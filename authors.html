About Author

Lily Feinn

Lily(she/her) is the associate managing editor at DeCapy, where she's been a writer, editor and project manager for nearly six years. She lives in New York with her bossy rescue dog, Darcy. When Lily's not obsessing over animals, you can find her writing comics and playing competitive pinball.
